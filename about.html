Our History

Kwekwe Polytechnic was established to serve as a center of technical and vocational excellence, training thousands of students in various fields of engineering, business, and technology.

Over the years, the polytechnic has become a hub of innovation and creativity, producing graduates who contribute meaningfully to national development and industry growth.

Our Mission

To provide quality education and training that empowers students with the knowledge, skills and attitudes necessary for personal and national development.

Our Vision

To be a leading polytechnic in Zimbabwe and beyond, recognized for excellence in teaching, innovation and community engagement.
